About this tool

Paste a list and instantly strip out repeated lines while keeping the first occurrence of each. Original order is preserved, which makes it safe for deduplicating keyword lists, email lists, log excerpts, or config entries.

How to use

  1. Paste your list into the Text field, one item per line.
  2. Review the deduplicated output, which keeps lines in their original order.
  3. Copy the cleaned list.

Frequently asked questions

Is matching case-sensitive?

Yes. "Apple" and "apple" are treated as different lines. Run the text through the Case Converter first if you want case-insensitive deduplication.

Do leading or trailing spaces affect matching?

Yes, lines must match exactly, so "apple" and "apple " are considered different. Use the Whitespace Remover to normalize spacing beforehand.

Which copy of a duplicate is kept?

The first occurrence. Later repeats are dropped, and the remaining lines stay in their original order rather than being sorted.

Are blank lines deduplicated too?

Yes. Empty lines are treated like any other line, so only the first blank line survives; the rest are removed.

Can I safely paste sensitive lists here?

Yes. Deduplication runs entirely in your browser with no upload, so email or customer lists never leave your device.
